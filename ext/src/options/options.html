TomCat CPR Google Voice Template Options

    Instructions:

    1. Be on the google voice site
    2. Select template to use from the extention popup by clicking on logo in the bar.
    3. Edit template with customers info then click insert. Info will be inserted into the text field if the google voice message.
    4. You can click send button or if further editing is needed edit in extention popup or click into the text field and press space then it will show the whole message.
    5. Send Message.
